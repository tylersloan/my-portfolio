EQUIP at North Hills

Equip is a yearly gathering of lay leaders in North Hills Community Church. It's a one-day event were pastors and mentors teach and encourage others to fight the good fight of their faith. In effort to fully equip attendees, I stamped the new logo on notebooks and screenprinted way-finding signage on plywood.
