House of Israel

House of Israel is a faith-based nonprofit organization founded in 2009. They had an old, tired, and confused identity. I helped update the copy, and designed a modern, comfortable identity that helped attract volunteers. The sleek new look also helps House of Israel stand out in the eyes local DSS case workers, who recommend the service to mothers and fathers who want to get their kids back.

House of Israel's website needs some love, too. It's functional as is, but is a chore to browse. I'm in the process now of designing & developing, simultaneously, a site that shows what HOI is all about, what they're doing today, and how you can help.
