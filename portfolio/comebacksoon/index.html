Come Back Soon for Tara-Leigh Cobble

Come Back Soon is Tara-Leigh Cobble's newest album, out in May 2013. The album was inspired on a trip to Israel, where Tara-Leigh photographed the sun in the sky, begging Jesus to come back soon. I set the hand-traced type face along with an arrow pointing down toward Earth to compliment Tara-Leigh's lyrics that praise the Lord with sincerety, passion, and personal touches. On the back I reversed the arrow to point back up to Heaven, adding a nice visual que for the verse.

Along with Tara-Leigh's album art, she requested some print promotional material. I updated some old things, created some new assets for her site, and provided her with a polished, modern page that she can now use to send to promoters instead of paper mail. The site also hosts downloadable resources promoters often use for upcoming events.
